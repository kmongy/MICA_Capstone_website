"You don't know what we've been through. You don't know what we had to overcome to have a good life here." These are words I've heard from my immigrant elders and parents on multiple occasions. Stories of life in the "old country" are often shared after that admonishment. Perhaps you've heard those stories before. Maybe you've heard it so many times that the stories are ingrained in you. That's certainly how I feel. And yet, I understand deeply how far removed I am from that experience.
